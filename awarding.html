Integri Fest 2024

Senin, 2 Desember 2024

Minister of Religious Affairs, Nasaruddin Umar, in his remarks, emphasized the importance of a strong commitment to eliminating gratuity practices within the Ministry of Religious Affairs that could harm the state and society. RAN PE AWARDS 2024

Senin, 19 Agustus 2024

Vice President of the Republic of Indonesia, K.H. Ma’ruf Amin, stated that the implementation of the first phase of the National Action Plan for Preventing and Countering Violent Extremism (RAN PE) has yielded positive results, including a decrease in terrorist attacks and an improvement in Indonesia's ranking in the Global Terrorism Index. These achievements are the result of strong collaboration and offer great hope for Indonesia, particularly in addressing violent extremism in the country.
