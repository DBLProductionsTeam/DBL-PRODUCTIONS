Launching Immigration Lounge Depok

Selasa, 21 Januari 2025

The Chairperson of the Religious Court of Depok, Honorable Mrs. Siti Salbiah, personally attended the event, which was also attended by several officials and leaders of institutions in Depok City, including the Mayor of Depok and the Chairperson of the Depok City Regional House of Representatives (DPRD). LAUNCHING EVENT

Launching Immigration Lounge Bekasi

Kamis, 19 Desember 2024

Acting Director General of Immigration, Saffar M. Godam, officially inaugurated the Immigration Lounge at Grand Metropolitan Mall Bekasi on Thursday (December 19, 2024). Operated under the coordination of the Bekasi Immigration Office, the Immigration Lounge was established to address the increasing number of immigration service applicants in both Bekasi City and Bekasi Regency. This immigration service unit offers the public a new experience by prioritizing fast, convenient, and practical service principles.
